Tested Oregon Scientific sensors and scales:
 

433.92MHz Oregon Scientific sensors are worldwide available.
The maximum number of sensors per category is limited by the number of channels available on the sensors in each category.
For example: TEMP1 sensors have 3 channels and the THGR810 has 10 channels.
You can use for example 1 THR138 and 2 THC138 and 10 THGR810 sensors.
